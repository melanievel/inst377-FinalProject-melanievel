Welcome!

Issue

College students often struggle to maintain a good nutrition. Depending on their circumstances they may have issues with time management, finances and budgeting, cooking, etc., all which affect their ability to access nutritious meals.

Purpose

The purpose of this app is to facilitate college students' access to nutritious meals! The app allows users to find recipes that fit their needs. Users have the choice to add customizations to narrow down their recipe search. They can also see the ingredients needed, the time it takes to make the recipe, how much it would cost to prepare it, etc. These features allow the users to decide which recipes are a good fir for them based on how much time they have, their budget, their cooking skills, and more!

Resources
